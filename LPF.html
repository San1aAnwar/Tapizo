ABOUT US

Dedicated to uplifting dropouts since 2018, Learning Point Foundation works as an NGO providing quality education to underprivileged communities in Bengaluru's slum areas. We empower students to complete their 10th and 12th exams and help them secure job placements, paving the way for a brighter future. Our mission extends to educating and counseling women and teachers, ensuring holistic development for all.
